What is Dungeon Crawler Carl?

IMAGE FROM:goodreads

Dungeon Crawler Carl also known as DCC is a hit Lit Rpg series by Matt Dinniman. The series has 7 books with the last one having come out on November 11th 2024. This series was originally an indie pub, but it got so popular last year that it got picked up by TOR publishing and the series is now being published again with different covers. This series is also being turned into a live action movie that is slated to come out sometime in the next few years.
