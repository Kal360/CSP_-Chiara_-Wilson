What is Sundance?

Sundance is a gorgeous mountain resort with gorgeous powdery snow located in the tiny town of Sundance Utah. It is a small resort with lots of small and cozy cabins, great restruants,skiing and even theatre plays. It is also less crowed compared to other ski resorts is very pretty.
