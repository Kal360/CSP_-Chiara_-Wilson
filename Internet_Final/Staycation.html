What is Sundance?

Sundance is a gorgeous mountain resort with gorgeous powdery snow located in the tiny town of Sundance Utah. It is a small resort with lots of small and cozy cabins, great restruants,skiing and even theatre plays. It is also less crowed compared to other ski resorts and is a great place if you want a romantic getaway.

Image From: alpineadventures

Why should You go to Sundance?

  1. 1. It is in a very convinent location because its 15 mins from the town of Provo and less that an hour away from Salt lake city.
  2. 2. It is under the Iconic Mount Timpanogos Peak and if you reach the top of Red's ski lift to Bearclaw Cabin, youll will be treated with the grogeous views of both the Utah valley, Heber valley and the deer creek resevior.
  3. 3. There are other things to do apart from skiing! There is also, an art studio whre you can make Jewelry, pottery, soap, or candles, a spa where you can relax and even a winter zipline tour.
  4. 4. It has some great, cozy restruants including the romantic award-winning Tree Room, the rustic Foundry Grill with great brunch, and live music at the Owl Bar.
